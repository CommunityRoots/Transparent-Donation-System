Welcome to

Community Roots

Community Roots is a way for charities to highlight the needs of people who are urgently looking for help within your community. You can donate towards these needs. When the full amount has been raised the money will be released to the charity. This means you know where your money is going. This sites aim is to help those who need it. Since we are non-profit, you can donate knowing that all of the money you donate will go directly to the cause that you want to help.

The Process Explained

A charity will identify a person with a need in their area.


The charity will verify this is a genuine need. This will involve a home visit.


The charity will enter the need into the CommunityRoots system.


Users from all around the world can donate money to the need.


Transparent

You can see exactly what your money has been used for. We try to keep you updated at every step. We do everything we can to be open and have even released the code this site runs on. If you are tech-savvy, you can view it here.

Your money

You are in control of your money. You decide what your money goes towards. Your money will go to a real need, that can help the life of a real person.

We Care

Community Roots was built with one purpose, to help people. This is what we aim to do.

The Charities

Charities visit the homes of people with needs. They verify there is an actual need. This is how needs get onto our website. Without charities this system wouldn't function
