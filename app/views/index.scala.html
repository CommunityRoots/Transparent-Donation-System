Welcome to

Community Roots

Community Roots is a way for charities to post needs of real community members. You can then donate towards this need. When the full amount has been raised the money will be released to the charity. This means you know where your money is going. Your money will be used exactly how you want to help the needs of real people.

Transparent

You can see exactly what your money has been used for. We try to keep you updated at every step. The code that controls this system is also online and can be viewed here.

Your money

You are in control of your money. You decide what your money goes towards. Your money will go to a real need, that can help the life of a real person.

We Care

Community Roots was built with one purpose, to help people. This is what we aim to do.

Our Partners

Charities visit the homes of people with needs. They verify there is an actual need. This is how needs get onto our website. Without charities this system wouldnt function
